**Cultivation Time:** 15,16 January 2025 – June 2025.

**Cultivated variety:** Field leader, Big boss.

**Problems & probable causes:**

*   Leaf feeder: Lack of quality insecticide.
    
*   Shoot & fruit borer: High temperature, latency in establishment of pheromone trap & lack of quality insecticides.
    
*   Gemini virus: Poor management of white fly.
    
*   Phomopsis blight: Heavy rainfall.
    
*   Death of plants: Poor drainage system & heavy rainfall.
    

**Prevention of problems:** 

*   **Leaf feeder: Use of quality insecticide.**
    
*   Shoot & fruit borer: Timely establishment of pheromone trap with appropriate management & use of quality insecticide.
    
*   Gemini virus: Maintenance of ETL(Economic threshold level) of white fly & use of various quality insecticides.
    
*   Phomopsis blight: Spray good graded fungicides after rainfall.
    
*   Death of plants: Ensure a good drainage system
