**Cultivation Time:** 16 April 2025 – June 2025 

**Cultivated variety:** Chokka super.

**Problems & probable causes:**

*   Fruit fly: Delaying in tarzan trap.
    
*   Downy mildew: Heavy rainfall.
    
*   Mosaic virus: High temperature and white fly infestation.
    
*   Death of plants: Water logging condition. 
    

**Prevention of problems:** 

*   **Fruit fly: Providing tarzan traps in time & ensuring use of quality insecticides.**
    
*   Downy mildew: Using appropriate fungicides after rainfall.
    
*   Mosaic virus: Maintenance of ETL(Economic threshold level) of white fly & use of various quality insecticides.
    
*   Death of plants: Ensure a good drainage system.
