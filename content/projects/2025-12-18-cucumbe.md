**Cultivation Time:** 15 January, 2025 – 30 March, 2025 

**Cultivated variety:** Thushara riz.

**Problems & probable causes:** 

*   Latency in seedling emergence: Caused due to heavy dew, low temperature & clay type soil. 
    
*   Downy mildew: Extreme weather condition.
    
*   Fruit fly: Infestation rate was higher due to lack of quality insecticide and being not able to use tarzan traps in due time.  
    
*   Fruit shape: Deformity in fruit shape.
    
*   Death of all plants: Late sowing of winter variety.
    

**Prevention of Problems:** 

*   Latency in seedling emergence: Avoiding cultivating in clay soil, use of appropriate amount of compost with clay soil & cover the seed area with straw.
    
*   Downy mildew: Using appropriate fungicides.
    
*   Fruit fly: Providing tarzan traps in time & ensuring use of quality insecticides.
    
*   Fruit shape: Spray boron & zinc in proper time.
    
*   Death of plant: Choosing appropriate variety for time, ensuring proper fertilization and PGR application.
    

**Success:**

*   Downy mildew was managed efficiently.
    
*   Fresh harvest cost
