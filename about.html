About Us

Our objective is to channel cash flow from urban to rural areas to mitigate the cash flow shortage in rural regions, specifically in the agricultural sector. We aim to transform agriculture into an investable industry and make microfinancing risk-free for farmers.

Our business aims to contribute to societal improvement by addressing critical issues in both the agricultural and financial sectors.

For Farmers: Zero-Risk Scheme

We make micro-financing completely risk-free through our “Zero-Risk Scheme”, based on a profit-sharing model and post-harvest repayment. This empowers farmers to access necessary financing for high-yield production without fear of financial loss—improving agricultural productivity and livelihoods.

For Investors: Halal Investment Pool

We offer the “Halal Investment Pool”—a unique, Shariah-compliant investment product delivering inflation-beating returns. It serves as a stable alternative to volatile stock markets, underdeveloped bond markets, and low-yield bank deposits, meeting the demand for ethical and sustainable investing.

Our Broader Impact

Through community development initiatives, we reduce agricultural production costs—further boosting farmers’ income and fostering inclusive economic growth. By linking risk-free rural financing with ethical urban investment, we drive sustainable development that benefits both rural and urban communities.
